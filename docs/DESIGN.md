PixelMon Journey is **not** a website.

## 2. Simplicity

Do not overload the player.

The world is the main attraction.

UI exists only to support the journey.

---

## 3. Consistency

Every scene must look like it belongs to the same universe.

Never mix different pixel sizes.

Never mix lighting styles.

Never mix perspective.

---

## 4. Motion Creates Life

Nothing should feel frozen.

Examples

Fog slowly moves.

Moon glows.

Fireflies float.

Lanterns flicker.

Grass sways.

Creature breathes.

Small movement creates immersion.

---

## 5. Discovery

Portfolio information should be discovered.

Never dump everything on screen.

Reward scrolling.

Reward curiosity.
